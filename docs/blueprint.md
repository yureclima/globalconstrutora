# **App Name**: Global Construtora Showcase

## Core Features:

- Institutional Content Display: Showcase company information, 'About Us', 'Services', and 'Differentiators' sections with compelling visuals and text.
- AI Image Enhancement Tool: A tool that automatically suggests and applies visual treatments (cropping, brightness, contrast adjustments, color overlays, and rounded borders) to uploaded project images, ensuring a consistent, professional, and visually appealing gallery.
- Dynamic Project Gallery: An interactive section to display completed construction projects with high-quality, treated images, titles (e.g., 'Área de lazer', 'Reforma'), and brief descriptions in a modern grid layout.
- Client Testimonials Showcase: A dedicated section to feature client testimonials, providing social proof and building trust with potential customers.
- WhatsApp Integration & CTA: Implement fixed and prominently placed WhatsApp buttons across the site, including main calls-to-action, pre-filling messages like 'Olá, vim pelo site da Global Construtora e gostaria de solicitar um orçamento.'
- Quick Quote Request Form: A simple contact form to capture leads including fields for name, WhatsApp, city, service type, and a message, submitting to an external service for easy management.
- SEO & Analytics Foundation: Implement core SEO elements (title, meta description, keywords), and integrate Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, and Meta Pixel, along with tracking for key conversion events like WhatsApp clicks.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A strong and professional construction orange (#ED862D), embodying energy and authority.
- Background Color: A very light, desaturated warm off-white (#F8F3EE) that provides a clean and inviting canvas for content, subtly harmonizing with the primary orange hue.
- Accent Color: A deep, sophisticated red (#87213B), used sparingly for impactful highlights and calls to action, providing strong contrast while remaining analogous to the primary color's warmth.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable characteristics, aligning with a professional and authoritative brand image.
- Solid, geometric icons that are modern and precise, reflecting the company's strong, professional, and reliable construction ethos.
- A clean, well-organized, and highly responsive layout featuring large, impactful images, clearly defined content blocks, and strong calls-to-action, designed to work seamlessly on all devices.
- Subtle and smooth animations and transitions used sparingly to enhance user experience, such as elegant fades on image loading or smooth scrolling to sections, contributing to a polished and high-quality feel.
